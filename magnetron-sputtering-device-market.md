<p><ul><li>DC Magnetron Sputtering</li><li>RF Magnetron Sputtering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetron sputtering device market is primarily categorized into two types: DC magnetron sputtering and RF magnetron sputtering. DC magnetron sputtering is typically used for conductive materials, utilizing direct current to facilitate the deposition process. In contrast, RF magnetron sputtering employs radio frequency power, making it suitable for insulating materials, as it can efficiently ionize gas at lower pressures. Both methods serve various applications in the semiconductor, optical, and coating industries, enabling precise thin-film deposition.</p></p>
