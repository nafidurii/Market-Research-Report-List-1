<p><p>The thermal silica market encompasses three primary types: thermal grease, thermal paste, and thermal patches. Thermal grease is a viscous compound used to enhance heat transfer between surfaces, often in electronic applications. Thermal paste, similar in function, provides a more consistent layer for efficient thermal conduction, commonly seen in CPUs and GPUs. Thermal patches are solid or semi-solid materials pre-cut for easy application, ideal for specific areas where thermal management is critical, such as in high-performance electronic devices.</p></p>
