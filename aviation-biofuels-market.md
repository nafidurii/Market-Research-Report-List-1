<p><p>The aviation biofuels market primarily includes Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil (HVO) and Fischer-Tropsch (FT) fuels. HVO is derived from the hydrogenation of vegetable oils, providing a drop-in fuel compatible with existing engines, leading to reduced emissions and a sustainable alternative to conventional jet fuel. Fischer-Tropsch fuels are produced from biomass through gasification and subsequent chemical synthesis, creating a synthetic fuel with similar properties to jet fuel. Both options aim to enhance sustainability in the aviation sector and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
