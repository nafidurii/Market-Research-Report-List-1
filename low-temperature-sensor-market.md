<p><p>The low temperature sensor market includes several key types. Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) sensors decrease in resistance as temperature drops, making them ideal for precise temperature measurements. Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs) use metal resistance changes to provide accurate readings across a wide range. Thermocouples consist of two dissimilar metals, generating a voltage that relates to temperature differences, suitable for extreme environments. Other sensors include bimetallic and semiconductor types, catering to specialized applications across various industries, enhancing temperature monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
