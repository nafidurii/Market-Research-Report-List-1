<p><p>The Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer (SMA) market is typically divided into two types: A-SMA and R-SMA. A-SMA, or amphiphilic SMA, is often used in applications requiring high compatibility with different polymers and improved surface properties. R-SMA, or reactive SMA, is utilized for its enhanced reactivity, making it suitable for applications in adhesives, coatings, and modifications of other materials. These distinctions highlight their specific functional benefits and target applications within various industries, including automotive, packaging, and construction.</p></p>
