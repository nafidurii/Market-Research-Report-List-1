<p><p>The non-invasive glucose monitoring system market comprises two primary types: wearable devices and non-wearable devices. Wearable devices, such as smartwatches or patches, continuously track glucose levels through sensors on the skin, providing real-time data and facilitating easy monitoring. Non-wearable devices, on the other hand, include handheld gadgets and diagnostic tools that measure glucose levels without skin contact, typically using techniques like spectroscopy. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management by eliminating the need for traditional blood-sampling methods.</p></p>
