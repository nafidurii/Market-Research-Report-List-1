<p><p>The artificial membranes market comprises various types, including regenerated cellulose, cellulose acetate, polyacrylonitrile, and ethylene-vinyl alcohol copolymer, along with others. Regenerated cellulose membranes are known for their biocompatibility and high permeability. Cellulose acetate membranes are favored for their durability and filtration capabilities. Polyacrylonitrile membranes offer strong mechanical properties and chemical resistance. Ethylene-vinyl alcohol copolymer membranes are used in applications requiring barrier properties. Each type caters to specific industries such as biomedical, water treatment, and separation technologies.</p></p>
