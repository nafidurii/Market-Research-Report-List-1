<p><p>Tumor Cryoablation utilizes specialized catheters to destroy cancerous tissues through extreme cold. The market features two primary types: single point and multielectrode catheters. Single point catheters target a specific tumor area, delivering concentrated freezing directly to the lesion, suitable for smaller tumors. In contrast, multielectrode catheters have multiple points of contact, enabling simultaneous freezing across larger or irregularly shaped tumors, enhancing treatment efficiency. Both types are crucial for personalized cancer treatment strategies, improving patient outcomes and expanding therapeutic options.</p></p>
