<p><p>Height adjustable medical beds are categorized into two main types: electric medical beds and manual medical beds. Electric medical beds utilize a motorized mechanism, allowing users to effortlessly adjust the height and position with the push of a button. This provides enhanced convenience and comfort for both patients and caregivers. In contrast, manual medical beds require users to manually adjust the bed height using a crank or lever, offering a more cost-effective solution, albeit with less ease of use. Both types serve essential roles in healthcare settings.</p></p>
