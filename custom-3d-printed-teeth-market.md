<p><p>The custom 3D printed teeth market primarily includes two types: complete dentures and partial dentures. Complete dentures replace all missing teeth in an arch, providing a functional and aesthetic solution for edentulous patients. In contrast, partial dentures are designed to fill gaps left by one or more missing teeth while preserving the remaining natural teeth. Both types benefit from 3D printing technology, allowing for precise customization, faster production times, and improved comfort and fit for patients.</p></p>
