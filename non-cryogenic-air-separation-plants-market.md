<p><p>Non-Cryogenic Air Separation Plants utilize various technologies to separate air into its components without extreme cooling. Selective adsorption employs solid materials that capture specific gases, allowing others to pass through, effectively concentrating desired components. Differential permuation through membranes involves the use of selective membranes that permit certain gases to permeate at different rates, facilitating separation based on molecular size or properties. Other methods may include techniques like pressure swing adsorption or vacuum swing adsorption, each optimized for specific applications in gas production.</p></p>
