Adhesive systems rely on chemical bonds, using epoxy or resin-based adhesives to attach materials, providing seamless, vibration-resistant connections. Both systems cater to diverse applications, with mechanical fasteners excelling in heavy-duty scenarios and adhesives offering flexibility and aesthetic appeal in lighter, precision-based installations.</p></p>
