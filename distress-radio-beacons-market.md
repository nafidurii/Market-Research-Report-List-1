<p><p>Distress radio beacons are critical safety devices used in emergencies. The Personal Locator Beacon (PLB) is a compact, portable device primarily used by individuals for personal safety, transmitting distress signals to satellites. The Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacon (EPIRB) is designed for maritime use, activated in emergencies to help locate vessels in distress. The Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT) is used in aviation, automatically activated upon a crash to signal for rescue. Together, these devices enhance search and rescue operations across various environments.</p></p>
