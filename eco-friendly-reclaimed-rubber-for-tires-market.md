"Mechanochemical Regeneration" combines mechanical processing with chemical agents to enhance the rubber's performance and compatibility in new tire production. "Others" encompass alternative regeneration techniques, such as energy recovery and devulcanization processes, aiming to repurpose discarded tires while minimizing environmental impact, ensuring a sustainable approach to tire manufacturing.</p></p>
