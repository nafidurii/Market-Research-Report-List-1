<p><p>The silicone impression materials market is primarily divided into two types: Addition Silicone (A-Silicones) and Condensation Silicone (C-Silicones). A-Silicones are known for their superior accuracy, stability, and ease of use, making them ideal for detailed dental impressions and prosthetics. Both materials cater to diverse applications in dentistry, prosthetics, and industrial sectors, driving growth in the overall market.</p></p>
