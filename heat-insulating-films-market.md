<p><ul><li>Endothermic Film</li><li>Reflective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat insulating films market comprises two main types: endothermic films and reflective films. Endothermic films absorb thermal energy, thereby reducing heat transfer and maintaining cooler temperatures within enclosed spaces. In contrast, reflective films work by reflecting radiant heat away from surfaces, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort. Both types are commonly used in construction, automotive, and electronics industries to improve insulation and reduce energy consumption, contributing to sustainable practices and enhanced comfort levels in various applications.</p></p>
