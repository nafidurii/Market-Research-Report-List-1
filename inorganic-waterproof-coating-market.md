<p><p>The inorganic waterproof coating market is primarily categorized into two types: liquid and dry coatings. Liquid coatings are formulated for easy application and provide a seamless, protective layer against moisture infiltration. They are often used in construction and industrial sectors. Dry coatings, on the other hand, refer to powdered or solid forms that require activation, typically through heat or chemical processes. These coatings offer durable protection and are ideal for applications needing high resistance to water and environmental stressors.</p></p>
