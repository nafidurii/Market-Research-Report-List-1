<p><p>The electronic dosimeter market includes various types such as TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) and OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) dosimeters, along with other specialized dosimetry tools. TLD dosimeters utilize crystalline materials that emit light when heated, providing accurate radiation exposure measurements. OSL dosimeters employ light to stimulate luminescent materials, allowing for re-evaluation of stored data. Other dosimeters may include semiconductor-based or electronic personal dosimeters, catering to diverse applications in medical, industrial, and research settings for monitoring radiation exposure effectively.</p></p>
