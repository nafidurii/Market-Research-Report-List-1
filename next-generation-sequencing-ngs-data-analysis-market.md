<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) data analysis encompasses various technologies that enhance genomic research. Sequencing By Synthesis (SBS) technology involves detecting fluorescent signals as nucleotides are incorporated, enabling high-throughput sequencing. Single Molecule Real Time (SMRT) sequencing allows real-time observation of DNA polymerase activity, providing long reads and high accuracy. Ion Semiconductor sequencing leverages changes in pH as nucleotides are added, offering rapid results. Other emerging technologies include nanopore and ligation-based sequencing, expanding the capabilities and applications of NGS.</p></p>
