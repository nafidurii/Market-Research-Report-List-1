<p><p>The container pumps market includes various types designed for specific applications. Canister pumps are compact and portable, ideal for transferring liquids in small quantities. Siphon pumps utilize gravity to move fluids from one container to another, often used for fuel or water transfer. Hand pumps are operated manually, providing versatility for tasks where electricity is unavailable. Foot pumps rely on pedal action, commonly used in applications like inflating items or transferring fluids in constrained spaces. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
