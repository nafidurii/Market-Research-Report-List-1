<p><strong>The Ion Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Map Imaging</li><li>2D Slice Imaging</li><li>3D Ion Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion imaging system market encompasses various types, including velocity map imaging, 2D slice imaging, and 3D ion imaging. Velocity map imaging focuses on capturing the speed and direction of ions in a given space, enabling detailed kinetic studies. 2D slice imaging provides a planar view of ion distributions, offering insights into specific layers of samples. In contrast, 3D ion imaging allows for comprehensive volumetric analysis, facilitating a deeper understanding of complex ion interactions and distribution patterns in diverse materials.</p></p>
