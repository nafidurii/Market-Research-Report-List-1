<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis</li><li>Vertical Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore wind turbine market primarily consists of two types: horizontal axis and vertical axis wind turbines. Horizontal axis turbines are the most common, featuring blades that rotate around a horizontal shaft and are typically more efficient in energy capture due to their design. In contrast, vertical axis wind turbines have blades that rotate around a vertical shaft, offering advantages in turbulent wind conditions and easier maintenance. Both types cater to different offshore environments and energy needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
