<p><p>The ping-pong bat market primarily features two types: Shakehand and Penhold. Shakehand bats resemble a handshake grip, offering versatility and allowing players to easily switch between offensive and defensive styles. This type is popular among casual and competitive players alike. In contrast, Penhold bats are gripped similarly to holding a pen, providing enhanced control and wrist movement. This style is favored by players who prioritize quick spins and precise shots, especially in fast-paced games. Together, they cater to diverse playing preferences.</p></p>
