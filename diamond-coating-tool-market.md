<p><p>The diamond coating tool market is primarily divided into two types: natural diamond and artificial polycrystalline diamond (PCD). Natural diamond tools are prized for their hardness and performance in precision applications, making them suitable for demanding machining tasks. On the other hand, artificial PCD tools are manufactured by sintering diamond particles under high pressure and temperature, offering enhanced durability and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to various industries, including manufacturing and aerospace, optimizing cutting, drilling, and grinding processes.</p></p>
