<p><p>The automotive SoCs (System on Chips) market can be categorized into three main types: digital, analog, and mixed-signal. Digital SoCs focus on processing digital signals and enabling advanced functionalities like infotainment and driver assistance systems. Analog SoCs handle continuous signals, crucial for sensing and control tasks in vehicles. Mixed-signal SoCs combine both digital and analog elements, offering a versatile solution for applications that require processing of diverse signal types, enhancing overall vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
