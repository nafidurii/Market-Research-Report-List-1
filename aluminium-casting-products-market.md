<p><strong>The Aluminium Casting Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Molding</li><li>High Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Low Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Gravity Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum casting products market encompasses various casting methods, each suited for specific applications. Permanent molding uses reusable molds for high-volume production, ensuring durability and dimensional accuracy. High pressure die casting produces complex shapes quickly by injecting molten aluminum into high-pressure molds, ideal for intricate designs. Low pressure die casting utilizes a low-pressure system to fill molds, providing excellent quality and reducing defects. Gravity die casting relies on gravity to pour aluminum into molds, making it suitable for large components with good surface finish.</p></p>
