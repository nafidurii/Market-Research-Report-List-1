<p><strong>The Yarders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standing Skyline</li><li>Running Skyline</li><li>Live Skyline</li><li>High Lead</li><li>Jammer & Tong Thrower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Yarders are specialized logging systems categorized by their operational methods. **Standing Skyline** utilizes a fixed cable anchored high on a slope. **Running Skyline** involves a moving cable, allowing for increased logging efficiency. **Live Skyline** uses continuously moving cables for better load control. **High Lead** employs a high hoist for lifting heavy logs. **Jammer** systems feature a winch mechanism for pulling logs uphill. Finally, **Tong Thrower** systems rely on tongs attached to the cable to grasp and lift the logs effectively. Each type enhances productivity in forestry operations.</p></p>
