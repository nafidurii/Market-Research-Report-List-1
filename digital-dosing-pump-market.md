<p><p>The digital dosing pump market comprises various types, including diaphragm pumps, peristaltic pumps, and others. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to control flow, offering precise dosing for various applications. Peristaltic pumps operate by compressing a flexible tube, providing gentle fluid transfer ideal for sensitive liquids. Other types may include gear pumps and screw pumps, catering to specialized needs. Each type is essential for accurate chemical dosing in industries like water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
