<p><strong>The Oral Antidiabetic Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfonylureas</li><li>Meglitinides</li><li>Biguanides</li><li>Thiazolidinediones</li><li>Alpha-glucosidase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral antidiabetic agents market consists of several key categories. Sulfonylureas stimulate insulin release from pancreatic beta cells, while meglitinides act similarly but with a quicker onset. Biguanides, primarily metformin, enhance insulin sensitivity and reduce liver glucose production. Thiazolidinediones improve insulin sensitivity but may have safety concerns. Alpha-glucosidase inhibitors slow carbohydrate absorption in the intestines. Each type offers unique mechanisms for managing blood glucose levels in individuals with type 2 diabetes, impacting treatment decisions and market dynamics.</p></p>
