<p><p>The commercial air humidifier market primarily features two types: vapor-type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor-type humidifiers heat water to create steam, effectively increasing humidity levels in large spaces while ensuring a sterile output. In contrast, water spray humidifiers disperse fine water droplets into the air, providing a more immediate humidification solution without heating. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal humidity levels in various commercial settings, contributing to comfort, product preservation, and health benefits.</p></p>
