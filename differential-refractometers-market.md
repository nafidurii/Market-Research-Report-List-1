<p><strong>The Differential Refractometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refraction Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li><li>Interference Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential refractometers are analytical devices used to measure refractive index differences in various materials. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Refraction Type**: Measures changes in refractive index directly by comparing light paths through samples.</p><p>2. **Reflective Type**: Utilizes reflected light patterns to determine refractive index variations.</p><p>3. **Interference Type**: Employs interference patterns of light waves to assess refractive index differences, providing high sensitivity.</p><p>Each type serves specific analytical applications across industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
