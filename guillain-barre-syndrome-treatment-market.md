Immunoglobulin therapy involves administering concentrated antibodies to help modulate the immune response, reducing the severity and duration of the syndrome. Plasma exchange involves removing and replacing the patient's plasma to eliminate harmful antibodies, promoting recovery. Both treatments aim to alleviate symptoms and accelerate patient recovery, catering to a growing demand in the healthcare sector for effective interventions against this neurological condition.</p></p>
