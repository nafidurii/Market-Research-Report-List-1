<p><strong>The Bio PE Pouches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Fill</li><li>Cold Fill</li><li>Ambient Fill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio PE pouches are categorized based on their filling processes: hot fill, cold fill, and ambient fill. Hot fill involves pouring heated product into pouches to eliminate bacteria, making them suitable for high-temperature contents like sauces and juices. Cold fill is used for products requiring refrigeration, such as dairy, ensuring freshness without heat processing. Ambient fill pouches are designed for items that can be stored at room temperature, extending shelf life without refrigeration, ideal for dry goods and snacks.</p></p>
