<p><p>The automotive alarms market is primarily divided into two types: passive and active car alarms. Passive car alarms automatically activate when the vehicle is turned off and the doors are locked, providing seamless protection without user intervention. Active car alarms, on the other hand, require manual activation by the owner, often featuring remote controls or key fobs. Both systems aim to deter theft and enhance vehicle security, but they cater to different user preferences regarding convenience and control.</p></p>
