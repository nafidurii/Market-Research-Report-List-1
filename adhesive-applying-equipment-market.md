<p><p>The adhesive applying equipment market encompasses various types designed for precise adhesive application. Adhesive controllers manage the flow and dosage of adhesives to ensure uniformity. Cold glue applicators utilize water-based adhesives for eco-friendly bonding without heat. Pneumatic adhesive applicators use compressed air to enhance the efficiency and speed of application, ideal for high-volume production. Application guns offer versatility for manual or automated use, permitting controlled application in diverse settings, from manufacturing to crafting industries.</p></p>
