<p><strong>The Aircraft Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Type</li><li>Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Displacement Sensors market is categorized into two main types: Non-Contact and Contact types. Non-contact displacement sensors utilize techniques like laser or ultrasonic measurement to gauge distance without physical interaction, ensuring minimal wear and tear and enhanced durability. In contrast, contact sensors rely on direct physical contact to measure displacement, typically using potentiometers or linear variable differential transformers (LVDTs). Both types play crucial roles in aircraft systems, providing vital data for monitoring and control applications to ensure safety and performance.</p></p>
