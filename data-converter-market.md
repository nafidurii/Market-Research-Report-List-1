<p><p>The data converter market comprises two primary types: Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADCs) and Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs). ADCs transform analog signals, such as sound or light, into digital formats for processing in electronic devices, enabling applications in telecommunications, healthcare, and consumer electronics. Conversely, DACs convert digital data back into analog signals, crucial for sound playback, video rendering, and control systems. Both types are essential for bridging the gap between the analog and digital worlds, driving advancements in technology across various industries.</p></p>
