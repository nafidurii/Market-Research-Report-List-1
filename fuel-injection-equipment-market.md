<p><p>The fuel injection equipment market is primarily divided into two types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection (MFI). EFI utilizes advanced sensors and electronic controls to optimize fuel delivery, enhancing performance and efficiency in modern vehicles. In contrast, MFI relies on mechanical components to deliver fuel, offering simplicity and reliability, making it more common in older models and certain applications. Each system caters to different automotive needs, balancing efficiency, performance, and technology preferences in engine design.</p></p>
