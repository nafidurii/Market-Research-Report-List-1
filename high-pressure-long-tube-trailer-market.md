In contrast, bundled type trailers consolidate multiple gas cylinders into a single unit, optimizing space and enhancing safety during transport. Both types cater to industries requiring efficient gas transportation, with specific advantages in handling, capacity, and operational efficiency in high-pressure applications.</p></p>
