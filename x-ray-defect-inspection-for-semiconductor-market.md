Broadband Plasma Patterned techniques utilize broad-spectrum X-rays to detect irregularities across semiconductor layers. e-Beam Patterned inspection employs electron beams for high-resolution imaging of defects. Other methods may include advanced imaging techniques or hybrid approaches, all aimed at enhancing defect detection and improving semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
