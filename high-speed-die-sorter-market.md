<p><p>The high-speed die sorter market encompasses various types, including turret type and gravity type sorters. Turret-type sorters utilize multiple rotating trays to efficiently organize die castings based on specific criteria, enhancing sorting speed and accuracy. Gravity-type sorters rely on gravity to channel die castings through inclined planes, ensuring effective separation based on size or weight. Other types may include automated or robotic systems, expanding the technological landscape of die sorting solutions to meet industry demands for precision and efficiency.</p></p>
