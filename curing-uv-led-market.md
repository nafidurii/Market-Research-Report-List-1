<p><p>The Curing UV LED market is segmented into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs, predominantly used in applications like adhesive curing and printing, emit long wavelengths for effective cross-linking. UV-B LEDs offer medium wavelengths and are primarily utilized in medical therapies and certain curing processes. UV-C LEDs emit short wavelengths, primarily used for disinfection and sterilization, leveraging their germicidal properties. Each type serves unique applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
