<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automobile TPMS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automobile Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market comprises two main types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. </p><p>Direct TPMS uses sensors mounted on each tire to measure pressure in real-time, providing accurate readings and alerts for low pressure. Indirect TPMS, on the other hand, relies on existing wheel speed sensors to estimate tire pressure by monitoring changes in rotation and stability, making it less precise. Both systems enhance vehicle safety and fuel efficiency by ensuring optimal tire conditions.</p></p>
