<p><p>The electronic power transformer market is categorized into single-stage, two-stage, and three-stage systems, each defined by the number of conversion processes. Single-stage transformers directly convert electrical energy with high efficiency, suitable for low power applications. Two-stage transformers involve an intermediate conversion, enhancing flexibility and allowing for better performance in medium to high power needs. Three-stage transformers provide extensive regulation and control, making them ideal for complex applications like renewable energy integration and distributed generation systems.</p></p>
