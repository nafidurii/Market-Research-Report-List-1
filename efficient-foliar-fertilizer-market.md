<p><p>The Efficient Foliar Fertilizer market comprises various types, including Nitrogenous, Phosphatic, Potassic, and Micronutrients fertilizers. Nitrogenous fertilizers enhance plant growth by supplying nitrogen, essential for amino acids and proteins. Phosphatic fertilizers promote root development and flowering by providing phosphorus. Potassic fertilizers strengthen plants' resistance to stress and improve overall health through potassium. Micronutrients fertilizers supply essential trace elements like iron, zinc, and manganese, critical for plant metabolic functions. Together, these fertilizers improve agricultural productivity and crop quality.</p></p>
