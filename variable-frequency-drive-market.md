<p><p>The Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) market includes three main types: AC drives, DC drives, and servo drives. AC drives control the speed and torque of AC motors by varying frequency and voltage, making them ideal for applications like pumps and fans. DC drives regulate the speed of DC motors, offering precise control for industries such as manufacturing. Servo drives are designed for high-performance applications, providing precise control of position and speed in robotic and automated systems, ensuring accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
