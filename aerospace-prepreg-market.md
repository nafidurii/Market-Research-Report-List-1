<p><p>Aerospace prepreg refers to pre-impregnated composite materials, typically consisting of fibers such as carbon, glass, or aramid, embedded in a resin matrix. These materials are partially cured and stored for later use in manufacturing lightweight, high-strength components for the aerospace industry. Aerospace prepregs are widely used in aircraft structures, including wings, fuselages, and interiors, due to their superior strength-to-weight ratio, durability, and resistance to environmental factors.</p><p>The Aerospace Prepreg Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period.
