<p><p>The Bullet Resistant Auto Glass market features several types of materials, each offering unique protective qualities. Polycarbonate is lightweight and highly impact-resistant, ideal for security applications. Acrylic provides good clarity and is also lightweight but may scratch easily. Glass-Clad Polycarbonate combines the benefits of both materials, delivering superior protection while maintaining clarity. Poly-Vinyl Butyral (PVB) is used as an interlayer in laminated glass, enhancing strength and safety, making it a popular choice for bullet-resistant applications in vehicles.</p></p>
