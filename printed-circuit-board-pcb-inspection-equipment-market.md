<p><strong>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI)</li><li>Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) inspection equipment market primarily features two key types: Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI). AOI utilizes high-resolution cameras to visually inspect PCB surfaces, ensuring component placement, solder quality, and defect identification. Conversely, AXI employs X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints and internal structures, particularly critical for multilayer boards. Both methods enhance quality control in PCB manufacturing, reducing defects and improving overall reliability in electronic devices.</p></p>
