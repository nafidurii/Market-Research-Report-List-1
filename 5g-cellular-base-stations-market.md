<p><p>The 5G cellular base station market comprises four key types: </p><p>1. **Macro Base Stations**: Large installations providing extensive coverage for wide areas, ideal for urban and suburban regions.</p><p>2. **Small Cells**: Compact units that enhance capacity and coverage in densely populated areas, complementing macro stations.</p><p>3. **Pico Cells**: Smaller still, these target specific indoor areas, enhancing connectivity in buildings like malls and offices.</p><p>4. **Femto Cells**: Very small, often home-based, units improving indoor network quality for limited users. These types together optimize network performance and user experience.</p></p>
