<p><p>The glass forming mold market comprises three main types: Ordinary Cast Iron Mold, Alloy Cast Iron Mold, and Other Material Mold. Ordinary cast iron molds are cost-effective and suitable for standard production processes. Alloy cast iron molds, enriched with elements like nickel and chromium, offer enhanced durability and resistance to thermal stress, ideal for high-precision applications. Other material molds utilize diverse substances such as ceramics or plastics, catering to specific needs in niche markets and innovative glass products.</p></p>
