<p><p>The fall arrest safety net market can be categorized into two main types: knotted and knotless safety nets. Knotted safety nets consist of interwoven strands with knots at each junction, providing robust strength and stability, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, knotless safety nets use a continuous mesh design that enhances flexibility and reduces the risk of snagging, offering superior performance in situations requiring quick deployment and adaptability. Each type serves specific safety needs in various environments.</p></p>
